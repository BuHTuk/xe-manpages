Command:

xe vlan-destoy - Delete a VLAN from a Network

xe vlan-destroy uuid=<param>

Prerequisites:

xe vlan-list

Parameters:

uuid
    UUID of the VLAN to delete 

Description: 

xe vlan-destroy deletes a VLAN from a network. You must disable any actives VIFs connected to the VLAN.
